😊 Learn a little bit about me!

Growing up in Bangalore, Singapore, and the Bay Area—each a global hub of innovation and technology—I was naturally drawn to the energy of problem-solving and building things that matter. Whether it was the tech-forward culture of Bangalore, the digital infrastructure in Singapore, or the entrepreneurial spirit of Silicon Valley, I found myself inspired by the people around me who were using technology to shape the future. That environment sparked my curiosity and eventually led me to computer science as a way to combine creativity, logic, and real-world impact. At Cornell, I’ve pursued that passion through hands-on work with the Cornell Mars Rover team and robotics research in the EmPRISE Lab. I’ve also loved mentoring young girls in coding through Women in Computing, and applying machine learning in a real-world setting during my internship at HiPaas Inc. For me, CS is more than code—it’s a tool for exploration, equity, and empowerment.
